Tibsigner

Hi! I am Tibber, UI/UX Designer who creates optimal solutions for everyone. I believe that everything in the world has their own meanings. They can come from the main problem which they solved along with a lot of implicative meanings. As a designer, I have an aim to connect all meanings from a product to users, engineers and business.

Design process

In every situation, we still have problems. That is a signal for a growth. As a designer, I have a responsibility to discover yours and then solve it. To do that, I have to keep myself curious. Updating not only problems, but solutions from design trends and technical sides. To create a better experience, once again I have to keep myself curious and update user behaviour. Now, let’s discover my design process.

Competitor analysis

Before jumping to design a solution, I discover exist solutions to have an insight, learn from them, and then take first notes.

UX research

I grather data by talking to people, doing academic research and interview. I focus on their insights, create open conversations.

Analyze data

I use an affinity mapping to organize data into themes. After that, analyze them to figure out critical points. I focus on all aspects of user from problems, behaviours to their need and motivation.

Create personas

I divide and combine data of aspects from the previous step into meaningful data prefer to personas. I discover what they need, want, and problems.

Sketching solutions

I apply Crazy 8's method to quickly generate first solutions. Then, I ask questions: “How may we” to optimize these solutions.

Choose solutions

I once again use an affinity mapping to organize solutions into themes. I discuss/research to locate them to a prioritization framework. Thanks for my front-end knowledge, it helps me to make decisions much easier.

Create users journal

I create storyboards based on personas from each to each. I build a sitemap and then user flows.

Mid-fidelity

I use an UI Kit from Figma to quickly create a mid-fidelity.

Testing + iterate

I create a prototype by implement a feature from Figma. I use it to make a test to validate feature solutions. I take notes during the test to create a better version.

Brand style guide

I start with a message, missions and attributes and generate to fonts, colors, design’s rules, and style guide.

Patterns/Components

I create components from atoms (colors, fonts, icons,...).

High-fidelity

I combine components together to build high-fidelity mockups.

Interactive prototype

I implement features from Figma to create a closest version of product.

Usability testing

I separate steps to accomplish the goal to create Usability Tasks. I bring them along during the test to take notes and discover performance of each task. I learn from it and iterate the design.

Responsive design

I consider making a responsive design to match with user behaviour.

Trade-off

I list potential solutions along with the reason why I don’t choose them.

Hand-off

I use Zeplin to deliver all pieces of design to engineers.

Learning (Take-away)

After finish a project, I spend a few minutes to list all things I learnt which can help me in next projects.
